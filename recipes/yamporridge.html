Lasagna

Description

When I say this is an insanely delicious vegetable yam porridge, you have got to trust me on this one. Now is it porridge or pottage? that I cannot answer?. I grew up calling dishes made this way porridge so I will like to stick to that. I have tried to find out the meaning of both to be able to differentiate, but I have got myself even more confused than ever. I decided to stick to what my childhood memories tell me, which is porridge. Feel free to call it pottage.

Ingredients

  • 10 /11 in tuber of yam washed and cut into chunky size cubes
  • ¼ cup coarsely ground crayfish
  • 2 medium onions chopped and divided
  • smoked fish washed and deboned optional
  • 2 medium bell peppers coarsely blended
  • 1 habenero pepper blended with the red bell peppers
  • 1 Tbsp crushed garlic
  • ¾ cup palm oil
  • Salt or 2 bouillon cubes to taste
  • 12 oz kale or vegetable of choice chopped

Steps

  1. In a 6 quart stock pot put the yam chunks, add water to just about an inch below the level of the yam in the pot.
  2. Add in ½ of the chopped onions, crayfish, bouillon cube and bring to boil.
  3. AIn a medium saucepan, pour in the palm and heat up, then put in the other ½ of the chopped onions, sauté till transluscent, then pour in the coarsely blended bell pepper and habenero pepper mix. Cook for about 3 minutes or until the moisture evaporates.
  4. Turn off heat and wait for the yam to cook
  5. When the yam is soft and cooked, pour in the sauce you made stir in an Allow to simmer for another minute.
  6. Add in the chopped vegetables, stir in and turn off heat.
  7. Cover pot to allow the vegetables cook in the residual heat.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy
